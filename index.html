How to play

  • Tap a glowing job to send your character to do it.
  • Tap a family portrait (bottom) to switch who you control.
  • 👶 Elliot can't walk — tap him to carry him. Your hands are full while holding him, so plan the order: seat him, then fetch.
  • 🍼 Feeding needs Elliot in the highchair AND a bottle from the fridge.
  • 🎒 In later stages, find a baby carrier to hold Elliot and an item at once.
  • 🔑 Lost keys are under the couch — only Owen can crawl under!
  • Finish all the goals (top of screen) before the clock runs out… and don't let the Chaos meter explode. 💥
  • New here? Start with Stage 1 — just Dad. Stages unlock as you win.

On a computer: WASD/arrows to move, 1–4 to switch, space to act.
